Most Trusted Hair Transplant Clinic in India

Hair loss is a widespread problem that often has underlying medical causes. The DHI Technique™ (Direct Hair Implantation) offers a permanent solution for hair loss in India. This procedure is designed to address various factors contributing to baldness, such as genetics, stress, medical conditions, and external influences.

DHI™ India is one of the most trusted and renowned names, offering a unique technique for hair transplants in India. In the consultation, the DHI™ consulting doctor correctly diagnoses the area and the problem leading to baldness and hair loss and advises an appropriate hair loss treatment plan to give you a natural, dense look. This hair loss treatment is safe and offers permanent results. With the DHI Technique™ (Direct Hair Implantation), the follicles are extracted and implanted directly.

This provides total control of the angle, direction and depth of implantation, achieving much higher growth than FUE or FUT and gives natural-looking results.

For maximum safety, Direct Hair Implantation is performed exclusively with single-use instruments by doctors trained and certified by the London Hair Restoration Training Academy.

We deliver natural and permanent results for each of our cases. Depending on the harvesting of the donor site, our trained and certified surgeons customize and plan the whole surgery to offer you satisfactory results. We will be overwhelmed to serve you with hair transplants in India. Safe Hair Transplant

The world’s safest hair transplant procedure is performed in a sterile environment per comprehensive safety protocols. All procedures are performed only after proper diagnosis and medical tests.
